# Lab 09: Integrate an AI Agent with Foundry IQ

This folder contains the resources needed for the Foundry IQ integration lab.

## Contents

### Data Files (`data/`)

Three product information files for Contoso Outdoor, a fictional outdoor equipment company:

1. **contoso-tents-catalog.md** - Complete catalog of Contoso tents, from ultralight backpacking tents to large family camping tents
2. **contoso-backpacks-guide.md** - Comprehensive guide to Contoso backpacks, including daypacks, weekend packs, and expedition packs
3. **contoso-camping-accessories.md** - Full range of camping accessories including sleeping bags, cooking equipment, lighting, and safety gear

## Usage

These files should be uploaded to Azure AI Foundry as part of the knowledge base creation process described in the lab instructions.

### File Format Note

The files are provided in Markdown format (`.md`) for easy viewing and editing. When uploading to Foundry IQ in the lab:

- You can upload these files directly as `.md` files
- Or optionally convert them to PDF format before uploading
- The content will be indexed and searchable regardless of format

## Product Information Overview

**Contoso Outdoor** specializes in high-quality outdoor and camping equipment for:
- Backpacking and hiking
- Family camping
- Mountaineering and alpine climbing
- Ultralight trekking

The product catalog includes:
- **Tents:** 2-person to 6-person, 3-season and 4-season options
- **Backpacks:** 25L daypacks to 75L expedition packs
- **Sleeping Systems:** Sleeping bags and pads for all seasons
- **Camp Furniture:** Chairs and tables
- **Cooking Equipment:** Stoves, cooksets, and fuel
- **Lighting:** Headlamps and lanterns
- **Hydration:** Water filters and bottles
- **Navigation & Safety:** GPS units, first aid kits, emergency shelters
- **Accessories:** Trekking poles, solar panels, dry bags, multi-tools

## Sample Queries for Testing

Once your agent is connected to the knowledge base, try these queries:

1. "What types of tents does Contoso offer?"
2. "Tell me about the features of your waterproof backpacks."
3. "What's the difference between the Alpine Explorer and TrailMaster Lite tents?"
4. "What size backpack do I need for a 3-day hiking trip?"
5. "What camping accessories would you recommend for a weekend trip?"
6. "How much does the Alpine Air Pro sleeping pad cost?"
7. "What's the warranty on the Expedition Pro 65L backpack?"
8. "Do you have any 4-season tents suitable for winter camping?"
